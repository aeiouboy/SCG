Domestics Transportation Service

Full Truckload (FTL)

SCG Logistics has in its network more than 10,000 trucks and expertise in the transportation routes and the handling and transporting of several types of products. Full truck loads are appropriate for customers who want to transport large volumes of products to the destination in a short amount of time.

Scope :

  • Capacity and flexibility to serve peak demand
  • Nationwide coverage
Consolidated/ less-than truckload (LTL)

To facilitate the rising demands outside of Bangkok for the transportation of smaller volumes of goods, SCG Logistics offers consolidation services through its Central Distribution Center located in a central location with connections to all regions of Thailand, using state-of-the-art technology. The companyâ€™s regional distribution centers are able to distribute goods to the end-customers accurately and in a timely manner, no matter where they are located in Thailand.

Scope :

  • Analysis and planning of appropriate transport modes with cost efficiency in mind
  • Product Handling
  • Right-time, right-place product distribution and delivery

Distribution centers :

  • Central region: the Central Distribution Center situated in Wang Noi district, Ayutthaya province
  • Northern region: a regional distribution center in Chiang Mai province
  • Southern region: a regional distribution center in Songkhla and Surat Thani provinces
  • Northeaster region: a regional distribution center in Khon Khaen province
Bulk

:: Multi-modal transport

SCG Logistics is an expert in multi-modal transport combining different means of transport for customers who want to transport goods within Thailand or import or export goods, at competitive prices. We offer loss and damage protection policies throughout the transportation process. Our operations strictly adhere to rules and regulations on environmental impact. We also engage in community development and environmental protection activities. Multi-modal transport is also considered green logistics.

Scope :

  • Analysis and planning of appropriate transport modes with cost efficiency in mind
  • Management of the transfer of goods and stockpile using special equipment, product handling to prevent breakage/ loss and damage of goods
  • Environmental impact assessment and management in line with the local laws and regulations governing health, safety and security

Distribution centers :

  • Port in Sri Chang district, Chonburi province
  • Bor Pong jumbo jetty in Bor Pong sub-district and Tha Wat Bandai pier in Ayutthaya province
  • Laem Muang pier in Kantang sub-district, Trang province
  • PC/ PK pier in Baang Goong district, Surat Thani provice
